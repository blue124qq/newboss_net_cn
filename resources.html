Methods of earning


Browsing websites, videos, ads

One thing you need to do is browse websites, videos and ads. You don't need to create accounts or write anything, just browse and discover ads. If nothing on your part will happen (lack of activity), then the monetization will be interrupted and you won't receive a certain amount of money.

Leaving engagement on social media

If you have an account on social media, this method might be very profitable for you. All you need to do is to leave likes, followers, shares, subscribers and more – for different people and pages you may like. If you don't have an account on one of the social media, you will have to set it up quickly.

Writing comments, reviews

Your writing skills will be a key here. It's not enough to just write anything, but you'll have to make an effort and write extended sentences, otherwise, you won't receive your pay. In some sections, you'll already have prepared reviews or comments to copy and paste. Your creativity will be rewarded.

Filling out surveys

Just complete surveys which other people published. Let them know about your personality and make this world for better. Surveys can be different, but they will be matched to your chosen interests on Zareklamy. Some surveys are anonymous and some public. Information about that will be displayed to you.

Creating accounts, subscribing to newsletters

You'll get the most money for this method of earning, but it's associated with great commitment. All you need to do is create accounts on various websites, where you have to provide your personal details. The pages will be based on your interests and personality.

Monetization tips


Solving the problem with applications

This resource is essential for earning by leaving engagement on social media or writing reviews and comments. During monetization, the applications that you have can block you from opening browser windows, so we've provided a simple tutorial on how to change it.

Invite people and earn - Referral program

To increase your earnings on Zareklamy, use the Referral program. If the invited person makes a payment for the purchase of advertisements on Zareklamy Ads, you will receive a certain amount from the person's payment. Learn more about how to start making money by inviting people.
