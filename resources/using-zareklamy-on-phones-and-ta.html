Solving the problem with applications

Monetization on phones and tablets - earn correctly

Explanation

This resource is essential for earning by leaving engagement on social media or writing reviews and comments. During monetization, the applications that you have can block you from opening browser windows, so we've provided a simple tutorial on how to change it.

Android devices tutorial

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Scroll to bottom and click Apps.
  3. Find your application and click on them (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube etc.).
  4. Click Set as default.
  5. Click Go to supported URLs.
  6. Change In this app to In other app.

In "Settings" choose "Apps"

Choose the application with which you have a problem

In your app click "Set as default"

Then click "Go to supported URLs"

Change "In this app" to "In other app"

That's all!
