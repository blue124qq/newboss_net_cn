How are opinions written?

When creating an ad, you will need to enter the price you will pay customers for each Upwork review. The rate for the opinion depends on the selected target group. For example, when paying for reviews written by people from the United States, you will pay more than people from Venezuela.



Writing opinions from the customer's view

Let's say that hiring you for one hour costs $10. The person writing the opinion will have to pay this amount first. After one hour of hiring you and writing an opinion customer will get money back and receive a salary for it. Also, this customer will inform you that is from Zareklamy. You do not have to do a service for him.



Origin of recipients

If you created an ad on Zareklamy Ads, it will be displayed to targeted users on the second page - Zareklamy. Customers that will see your advertisement will be paid for completing a specified action. Both sides benefit and advertisers narrow the relationship with the potential customers.
