Explanation of the method of earning and some advices.

Start making money in just 2 simple steps.

Introduction

It's a 2nd of 5 available methods of monetization on the Zareklamy website. This method of earning consists of leaving engagement on social media. To use this method of monetization, you do not need any additional personal skills. All you have to do is have a minimum 1 account on social media like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and more. Everything is done automatically. Remember that if you want to use this method of monetization, you must have an account on selected social media. If you choose, for example, Facebook Likes, then you must have a Facebook account. Our system precisely selects the ads which should you show, based on your interests. You're only looking at what is most interesting to you.

Your salary depends on the number of your engagement during this monetization. For every engagement, you left on the social media page, photo, the publication you will receive as much as the advertiser paid for each entry from certain people. The salary of this method of monetization is 0.05 - $0.50 for every engagement on social media.

What I need to do?

Choose the method of earning

  1. Sign in to your Zareklamy account.
  2. Click on the menu bar (which is on the top left) and Earn.
  3. Then click green Earn button, which is below "Leave engagement on social media" block.

There is also an information icon in the graphic on the top right. If you click on it, the block will show you additional information about this method of monetization, e.g., salary, what to do, how to earn efficiently. All information provided in this block is also written here, where you are reading this.

Start leaving engagement.

When you clicked green Earn button, you will be able to see different social media pages which you can choose and earn on them. You can select one of many social media pages like Facebook (Likes, Followers, Shares, Groups and Events joins), Instagram (Followers, Likes), Twitter (Followers, Likes), YouTube (Views, Likes, Subscribers) and much more. You can only do one action at a time. Things related to Facebook (Likes, Shares) belong only to Facebook and can't be linked to a different page like YouTube.

We may show you different types of social media content (in accordance with the Terms of Services), photos, pages, publications, videos - general everything related with social media advertising and content on them.

Let's say you chose the method of earning by Facebook Likes. Then you will be redirected to the final page. On this page, you will see several pages and posts in separated blocks. The only thing you have to do is click Like button on these pages or posts. You can exactly see who you leave a like, what page or post it is or eventually skip it if the content is not interesting enough. In the window where you liked the page or post will automatically turn off and a new one will appear. All pages and posts are displayed based only on your personal information such as interests, age, origin, gender etc.

For gurus

Helpful information

In order to earn anything in this method of monetization you must to have minimum one account of social networking sites such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube, LinkedIn and more.

If you left like for page, publication, photo or whatever and got money but later you unliked or unfollowed them, your estimated earnings can be deducted. These actions can negatively affect your final earnings.
